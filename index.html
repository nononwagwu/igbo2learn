About the Project

"My Igbo Roots" is a cultural awareness web platform created to celebrate and educate users about Igbo traditions, language, proverbs, and heritage.

Users can explore authentic content, from traditional stories and values to everyday Igbo phrases and idioms.

The goal is to preserve and promote Igbo culture among youth and the diaspora using interactive tools.

Embrace the roots, language, and values that define the Igbo identity.

Omenala anyị. Akụ na ụba anyị.

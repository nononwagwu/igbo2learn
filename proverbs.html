Igbo Proverbs

Wisdom from Our Ancestors

Here are some beautiful Igbo proverbs and their meanings.

“A na m egbu egbu, m na-alu ya.”

We fight evil by working on it together.

“Onye na-amaghị ebe mmiri bidoro mawa ya, amaghi ka ọ ga-esi kọọ ya n’ọsọ.”

He who doesn’t know where the rain began cannot know where it will end.

“Ihe onye metara, ya jiri ya buru.”

Whatever one brings upon themselves, let them carry it.
